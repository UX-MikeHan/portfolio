Hi, my name is Mike Han. Welcome to my portfolio site!

"AIR"

An app to reduce the waiting time at the airport. Users can collect points placed all over the airport and can exchange these points into benefits e.g.:

  • Lounge access
  • Internet on board
  • Free coffee
  • Discount in the duty free shop
  • ...

"Let's talk"

An app to learn or improve a (new) language. The users have the chance to:

  • Listen to podcasts
  • Add new words or phrases to the library
  • Build their own folders for special occasions or places
  • Chat with other students
  • ...

"User personas"

User personas. As a UX Designer one of my skill is to build User Personas, which are self explanatory and gives the observer a direct impression of the person.
